🍽 Animal Feeding Guide

🐾

Providing the right food helps stray animals stay healthy, energetic, and disease-free.
Here are some safe food options for different animals.

🐶

Dogs

✔ Good Food
Cooked Rice
Boiled Chicken
Boiled Eggs
Dog Biscuits
Fresh Water
✖ Avoid
Chocolate
Onions & Garlic
Grapes
Alcohol
🐾
🐱

Cats

✔ Good Food
Boiled Fish
Cooked Chicken
Cat Food
Fresh Water
✖ Avoid
Milk (Many adult cats cannot digest it well)
Chocolate
Onions
Raw Meat
🐾
🐄

Cows

✔ Good Food
Fresh Grass
Green Fodder
Hay
Clean Water
✖ Avoid
Plastic Bags
Spoiled Food
Chemical Waste
🐾
🐦

Birds

✔ Good Food
Millets
Rice Grains
Seeds
Fresh Water
✖ Avoid
Salty Snacks
Chocolate
Moldy Food
🐾
ℹ

Important Tip:

Always provide clean drinking water along with food and keep the feeding area clean.
Small acts of kindness can save many lives!

🚑 What To Do If You Find an Injured Animal?

1️⃣

Stay Calm

Do not panic or frighten the animal.

2️⃣

Keep Safe Distance

Approach slowly and avoid sudden movements.

3️⃣

Offer Water

Provide clean drinking water if the animal is conscious.

4️⃣

Call a Rescue Team

Contact a nearby veterinary clinic or animal rescue organization.

5️⃣

Do Not Force Feed

Never force food into an injured animal's mouth.

❤️ Daily Animal Care Tips

🌞

Summer Care

Keep bowls of clean water outside for stray animals.

🌧

Rainy Season

Provide dry shelter using cardboard or plastic sheets.

❄

Winter Care

Use blankets or straw bedding to help animals stay warm.

💉

Vaccination

Support vaccination and sterilization drives in your community.

♻

Clean Feeding Area

Always clean the feeding area after serving food.

🐾

Be Patient

Stray animals may take time to trust people. Approach gently.
